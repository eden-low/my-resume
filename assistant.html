Before you start

When you ask a question, the Atlas Assistant sends your message and short excerpts from the EdenAtlas data scopes you've selected (never full images, exact coordinates, Finance, or other people's content) to a configured Qwen AI service to generate an answer. This only happens when you send a message — never just by opening this page.

This assistant is read-only: it cannot create, edit, delete, publish, or share anything on your behalf.
